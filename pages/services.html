Our Services

Mobile Rust Control

Pro Fleet Care Simcoe County provides mobile rust control services that come directly to your location. Our specialized process creates a barrier of protection for metals and neutralizes the harmful effects of salt and moisture.

As a mobile service, we eliminate the need for you to transport your vehicles or equipment to a shop. This saves you time, reduces downtime, and allows us to service your entire fleet at once, minimizing disruption to your operations.

We work around your schedule, offering evening and weekend appointments at no additional cost. This flexibility ensures that your business can continue to run without interruption while we provide the rust protection your vehicles and equipment need.

Unique Two-Step Process

Our rust control application involves a specialized two-step process that provides comprehensive protection:

Step 1: ROC 40 (Light Oil)

  • Penetrates seams and crevices
  • Ideal for interior panels
  • Reaches hard-to-access areas
  • Displaces moisture

Step 2: ROC 50 (Drip-less Oil)

  • Provides extra protection
  • Applied to high traffic areas
  • Protects frame rails and wheel wells
  • Creates a lasting barrier

This comprehensive approach ensures that all areas of your vehicle or equipment receive the appropriate level of protection, from the most exposed surfaces to the hidden interior components.

Areas of Protection

Our comprehensive protection covers all vulnerable areas of your vehicles and equipment:

Exterior Protection

  • Undercarriage
  • Fenders and doors
  • Door posts and hood
  • Trunk and roof line
  • Door handles and mirrors
  • Bumper rails and window trim

Interior Protection

  • Electrical wiring and components
  • Engine compartment
  • Interior panels
  • Frame rails
  • Wheel wells
  • Seams and channels

Our product is specially formulated to penetrate hard-to-reach areas, defying gravity to creep upwards, sideways, and downward. This ensures complete coverage and protection, even in areas that are typically difficult to access.
