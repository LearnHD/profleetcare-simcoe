Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our rust control services

General Questions

What is Pro Fleet Care?

Pro Fleet Care is a mobile rust control service provider specializing in the protection of vehicles and equipment from rust and corrosion. We come to your location to provide professional rust control treatments.

Why choose Pro Fleet Care?

We offer mobile service, professional application, comprehensive coverage, and proven results. Our service is convenient, thorough, and backed by years of experience in rust control.

Do you service all of Simcoe County?

Yes, we provide mobile rust control services throughout Simcoe County, including Barrie, Orillia, Collingwood, Midland, and surrounding areas.

Service Questions

How long does the treatment take?

Treatment time varies depending on the size and type of vehicle or equipment. Most treatments take between 30-60 minutes to complete.

How often should I get rust control treatment?

We recommend annual treatments for optimal protection. Regular maintenance helps prevent rust formation and extends the life of your vehicles and equipment.

What types of vehicles do you service?

We service all types of vehicles and equipment, including cars, trucks, trailers, heavy equipment, farm machinery, and industrial equipment.

Technical Questions

What products do you use?

We use a specialized rust control formula that penetrates deep into seams and crevices, providing long-lasting protection against rust and corrosion.

Can you treat vehicles that already have rust?

Yes, while our treatment works best as a preventive measure, it can also help slow down existing rust progression and protect unaffected areas.

Is the treatment safe for my vehicle?

Yes, our treatment is safe for all vehicle components, including paint, rubber, and plastic parts. It's specifically designed for automotive and industrial applications.
