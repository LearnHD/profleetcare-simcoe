Industries We Serve

Specialized Rust Control for Every Industry

Pro Fleet Care Simcoe County provides specialized rust control services for a wide range of industries. Our mobile service and unique application process can be tailored to meet the specific needs of different types of vehicles and equipment.

No matter what industry you're in, if you have vehicles or equipment that are exposed to moisture, salt, or corrosive materials, our rust control services can help extend their lifespan, reduce maintenance costs, and maintain their appearance.

Farm Equipment

Agricultural equipment is constantly exposed to harsh conditions that can accelerate corrosion. From moisture in the fields to fertilizers and chemicals, farm equipment faces numerous corrosion threats.

Our rust control services help protect:

  • Tractors and combines
  • Plows and tillers
  • Sprayers and spreaders
  • Balers and harvesters
  • Grain handling equipment
  • Irrigation systems

By protecting your farm equipment from rust, you can extend its lifespan, reduce downtime during critical seasons, and maintain its value for future trade-in or resale.

Benefits for Farm Equipment

  • Extends equipment lifespan
  • Reduces maintenance costs
  • Prevents breakdowns during critical seasons
  • Protects hydraulic systems and electrical components
  • Maintains resale value
  • Deters rodents from chewing wires

Emergency Vehicles

Benefits for Emergency Vehicles

  • Ensures reliability when it matters most
  • Protects critical electrical systems
  • Extends vehicle service life
  • Reduces maintenance costs
  • Maintains professional appearance
  • Protects specialized equipment

Emergency vehicles must be ready to respond at a moment's notice. Reliability is paramount, and rust can compromise both function and appearance.

Our rust control services help protect:

  • Fire trucks and pumpers
  • Ambulances and paramedic vehicles
  • Police cruisers and specialty units
  • Rescue vehicles
  • Command and communication vehicles
  • Emergency response equipment

Our mobile service means we can come to your fire hall, police station, or emergency services facility, minimizing downtime for these critical vehicles.

Passenger Transportation

Passenger transportation vehicles face constant exposure to road salt, moisture, and environmental conditions that accelerate corrosion. Maintaining these vehicles is essential for safety, reliability, and professional appearance.

Our rust control services help protect:

  • School buses
  • Transit buses
  • Shuttle vans
  • Taxis and rideshare vehicles
  • Tour buses
  • Accessible transportation vehicles

Regular rust control treatment helps maintain the structural integrity of these vehicles, ensuring passenger safety while reducing maintenance costs and extending vehicle lifespan.

Benefits for Passenger Transportation

  • Enhances passenger safety
  • Reduces vehicle downtime
  • Extends fleet lifespan
  • Maintains professional appearance
  • Protects against road salt damage
  • Reduces maintenance costs

  • Prevents costly repairs

Snow Removal and Construction

Snow removal and construction equipment operate in some of the harshest conditions, with constant exposure to salt, moisture, and abrasive materials. These vehicles and equipment require specialized rust protection to maintain their functionality and value.

Our rust control services help protect:

  • Snowplows and salt spreaders
  • Excavators and backhoes
  • Bulldozers and graders
  • Loaders and skid steers
  • Dump trucks and cement mixers
  • Cranes and lifts

Regular rust control treatment helps prevent costly repairs, reduces downtime, and extends the service life of your valuable equipment.

Benefits for Snow Removal and Construction

  • Protects against salt and chemical exposure
  • Prevents hydraulic system failures
  • Reduces equipment downtime
  • Extends equipment lifespan
  • Maintains resale value
  • Protects electrical components

Beach Equipment

Benefits for Beach Equipment

  • Protects against salt water corrosion
  • Prevents sand abrasion damage
  • Extends equipment lifespan
  • Reduces maintenance costs
  • Maintains equipment functionality
  • Preserves equipment value

Beach equipment faces some of the most corrosive conditions, with constant exposure to salt water, sand, and sun. These harsh elements can quickly deteriorate equipment without proper protection.

Our rust control services help protect:

  • Beach cleaning equipment
  • Lifeguard vehicles
  • Beach maintenance vehicles
  • Watercraft and jet skis
  • Boat trailers and dollies
  • Beach access equipment

Our specialized rust control products create a barrier that protects against the corrosive effects of salt water and sand, helping to maintain equipment functionality and appearance.
