Our Story

Renee & Jan are the owner/operators of the Pro Fleet Care dealership in the Simcoe Region of Ontario. Having owned their own construction business, they understand the importance of quality workmanship and customer service.

While speaking with a friend, they learned about an opportunity to join the Pro Fleet Care team. Being business owners, they understand the havoc corrosion can cause a fleet owner and the importance of keeping up with maintenance on one's vehicles.

Both Renee & Jan are motivated, driven, people persons, who are dedicated to serve their customers. They enjoy spending time with their children while working around their hobby farm.

Their background in construction and business ownership gives them a unique perspective on the importance of maintaining equipment and vehicles. They understand firsthand the costs associated with equipment downtime and repairs due to corrosion.

As Pro Fleet Care franchise owners, they bring their commitment to quality and customer service to every job, ensuring that each customer receives the best possible rust protection for their vehicles and equipment.

About Pro Fleet Care

Pro Fleet Care is a mobile rust control service provider with over 35 years of experience in the industry. Founded in 1984 by Bob Lawrie, the company has grown into a franchise network across North America.

The company was built on the principle of providing exceptional rust control services that come directly to the customer's location. This mobile service model eliminates the need for customers to transport their vehicles and equipment, reducing downtime and increasing convenience.

Pro Fleet Care's unique two-step application process has been refined over decades to provide superior protection against corrosion. The company's specialized products are designed to penetrate hard-to-reach areas and create a barrier of protection that neutralizes the harmful effects of salt and moisture.

Today, Pro Fleet Care continues to expand its franchise network, bringing its proven rust control methods to more communities across North America. Pro Fleet Care Simcoe County is proud to be part of this network, bringing the same quality service and expertise to our local community.
