Our Rust Control Process

Unique Two-Step Application Process

Pro Fleet Care's rust control process is based on over 35 years of experience in the industry. Our unique approach provides comprehensive protection for your vehicles and equipment, extending their lifespan and reducing maintenance costs.

What sets our process apart is our specialized two-step application that ensures complete coverage and protection, even in hard-to-reach areas. Our mobile service brings this professional-grade rust protection directly to your location.

Step 1: ROC 40 Application

The first step in our process involves the application of ROC 40, a specialized light oil designed to penetrate deep into seams, crevices, and hard-to-reach areas.

Key Features of ROC 40:

  • Penetrates deep into seams and crevices
  • Ideal for interior panels and enclosed areas
  • Displaces moisture and prevents corrosion
  • Creeps into hard-to-reach areas
  • Creates a protective barrier against salt and moisture
  • Defies gravity, allowing it to creep upwards and sideways

ROC 40 is specifically formulated to reach areas that other rust control products can't access. Its ability to creep and penetrate ensures that even the most hidden areas receive protection.

Step 2: ROC 50 Application

The second step in our process involves the application of ROC 50, a specialized drip-less oil designed to provide extra protection in high-traffic and exposed areas.

Key Features of ROC 50:

  • Provides extra protection in high-traffic areas
  • Drip-less formula stays where applied
  • Ideal for frame rails and wheel wells
  • Creates a durable protective barrier
  • Resists wash-off from road spray and weather
  • Continues to protect even in harsh conditions

ROC 50 is specifically formulated to provide long-lasting protection in areas that are most exposed to salt, moisture, and road debris. Its drip-less formula ensures that it stays in place, providing continuous protection.

Areas of Protection

Our comprehensive rust control process provides protection for all vulnerable areas of your vehicles and equipment. Here's a breakdown of the key areas we protect:

Exterior Protection:

  • Undercarriage: Complete protection for the entire underside of the vehicle
  • Fenders and Wheel Wells: Areas most exposed to road salt and debris
  • Doors and Door Posts: Protection for door edges and internal structures
  • Hood and Trunk: Including internal surfaces and seams
  • Roof Line: Protection for roof seams and drainage channels
  • Exterior Trim: Door handles, mirrors, bumper rails, window trim

Interior Protection:

  • Frame Rails: Complete coverage of this critical structural component
  • Electrical Components: Protection for wiring harnesses and connections
  • Engine Compartment: Protection for vulnerable components
  • Interior Panels: Protection for hidden areas prone to corrosion
  • Seams and Channels: Protection for areas where moisture collects
  • Fasteners and Brackets: Protection for mounting points and hardware
